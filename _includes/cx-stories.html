What is the data saying?

Over 1.1 million survey responses per quarter — The public wants to give feedback

20 of 24 participating High-Impact Service Providers are reporting feedback data. Most responses coming from agencies focused on healthcare: CMS and VHA.

Three agencies provided feedback on the total service experience, 12 provided data from a contact center, and 10 provided data collected from a website.

Overall Satisfaction of 4.0 out of 5 across all services — Good but room to improve

Response to a Likert-scale question typically phrased: “I am satisfied with the service I received.”

Regression analysis suggests that Overall Satisfaction is driven most by Effectiveness, Ease, and Efficiency of the interaction or interface.

Average ‘Employee’ response of 4.3 — People matter in providing a good service experience

Response to a Likert-scale question typically phrased: “People I interacted with were helpful”.

Average Overall Satisfaction of web channels trail call centers by 0.3 points in terms of satisfaction, effectiveness, and ease. Average Trust responses are equal between the two channels.

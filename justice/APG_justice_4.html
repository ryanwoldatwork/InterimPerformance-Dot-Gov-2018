Goal Leader: Seth Adam Meinero, National Violent-Crime and Narcotics Coordinator, Executive Office for United States Attorneys

Goal Statement: Strengthen and recommit our efforts to reduce gun violence through the launch of Project Guardian. By September 30, 2021, the Department will provide to state law enforcement fusion centers biweekly reports 100% of the time on NICS denials that are reported to ATF, and increase the number of USAO federal firearms prohibitor records submitted to NICS by 10 percent.

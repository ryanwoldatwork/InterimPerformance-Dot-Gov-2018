Goal Leader: Tara Sweeney, Assistant Secretary, Indian Affairs

Goal Statement: By September 30, 2021, Indian Affairs, through the BIA Office of Justice Services, will meet and exceed the goals established by Executive Order 13898, emphasizing the coordination of data sharing with federal partners and ensure that 100 percent of BIA active missing and murdered cases are entered into the National Missing and Unidentified Persons System (NamUs).

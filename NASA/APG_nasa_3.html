Goal Statement: Commence lunar surface science investigations, technology, and exploration demonstrations to enable a sustainable lunar surface exploration strategy. By September 30, 2021, deliver NASA science and technology payloads to the awarded Commercial Lunar Payload Services (CLPS) provider(s) for delivery to the surface of the Moon.

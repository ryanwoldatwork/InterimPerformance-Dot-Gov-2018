Reduce Surface Transportation-Related Fatalities

Goal Leaders:

Nicole R. Nason, Administrator, Federal Highway Administration (FHWA)
Jim Mullen, Acting Administrator, Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA)
James Owens, Acting Administrator, National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA)

Goal Statement: Reduce overall surface transportation-related fatalities. By September 30, 2021, DOT will reduce the rate of motor vehicle fatalities to 1.01 per 100 million Vehicle Miles Traveled (VMT).

Department of Health and Human Services  

Health Security

Goal Leaders:

Stephen Redd, Director, Office of Public Health Preparedness and Response, Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
Rebecca Martin, Director, Center for Global Health, Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
Sally Phillips, Deputy Assistant Secretary, Strategy, Planning, Policy and Requirements, Office of the Assistant Secretary for Preparedness and Response (ASPR)

Goal Statement:

Increase capacity to prevent health threats originating abroad from impacting the United States. By September 30, 2019, HHS will contribute to increasing the surveillance, workforce, emergency management, and laboratory capacity of 17 partner countries.

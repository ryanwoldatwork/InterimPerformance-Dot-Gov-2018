Department of the Treasury  

{{page.title}}

Goal Leader: Timothy Gribben, Commissioner, Bureau of the Fiscal Service

Goal Statement: Create a modern, seamless, and cost-effective Federal payment experience for Americans by:

  • Reducing the number of paper checks printed by Treasury’s Bureau of the Fiscal Service to 49 million by the end of FY 2021, compared with 54 million in FY 2019; and
  • Achieving an electronic payment rate of 96.1 percent by the end of FY 2021, compared with 95.6 percent in FY 2019.

Department of Defense  

DoD Reform Savings

Goal Leader: Lisa Hershman, Acting Chief Management Officer

Goal Statement: The Department will drive transformational improvements of business processes, systems, and policies to increase effectiveness and performance while aligning and reprioritizing savings to support the National Defense Strategy (NDS). By September 30, 2021, create a long-lasting culture of innovation, empowerment, and improvement to reduce the cost of doing business throughout the Department.
